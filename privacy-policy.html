Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 22, 2026

Vaultio ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Vaultio app and website at www.vaultio.online.

1. Information We Collect

Vaultio is a self-storage application. All data you store — including passwords, notes, files, photos, and videos — is stored locally on your device or within your personal account. We do not collect, access, or transmit your stored content to any third-party servers.

2. Data Ownership

You retain 100% ownership of all data you store in Vaultio. Your information is never sold, shared, or used for advertising purposes.

3. Encryption

Your data is encrypted to protect it from unauthorized access. We use industry-standard encryption practices to keep your information secure.

4. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information beyond what you voluntarily provide
  • Location data
  • Browsing or usage tracking data sold to third parties
  • Financial information

5. Third-Party Services

Vaultio is distributed through Google Play. Google may collect certain device and usage information in accordance with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

6. Children's Privacy

Vaultio is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
